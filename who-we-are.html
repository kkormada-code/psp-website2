Who We Are

Focused on bridging capital with growth across Africa

Piston Securities Practice Ltd (PSP) is a licensed issuing house dedicated to helping businesses and investors access the capital markets with confidence. Our mission is to bring clarity to capital raising, empower issuers with tailored solutions, and foster investor trust through transparency and rigor. With deep expertise in structuring and compliance, PSP is the go-to partner for credible and efficient issuance of securities across Africa.

Fast Facts

  • Founded: 2025
  • Licensed by: Securities and Exchange Commission, Ghana
  • Focus: Capital markets advisory & issuance
  • Headquarters: Accra, Ghana
  • Specialty: Issuing House
